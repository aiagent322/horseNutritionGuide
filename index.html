Educational Tool — Not Veterinary Advice

This tool interprets label text only. It does not diagnose, treat, or prescribe. Always consult a licensed veterinarian or qualified equine nutritionist for horses with:

  • Laminitis or founder history
  • Insulin resistance (IR), EMS, or PPID/Cushing's
  • Colic symptoms or history
  • Rapid or unexplained weight change
  • Dental problems or choke risk
  • Tying-up / PSSM / EPSM

No feed brand is endorsed or criticized. Assessments are based solely on label text provided.
